WHAT WE DO

Our approach to farm logistics starts with path planning and automation of in-field operational decisions. This ensures that all activities and actions create an added value in the logistics chain by ensuring the right equipment is at the right place, at the right time. This way, when today’s smaller groups of heavier machines are replaced by large swarms of smaller autonomous machines, Verge’s fleet management and logistics orchestration products will also play a key role in increasing their collective work rate.
