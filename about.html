About us

Focused on technology that's accelerating the transition to autonomous agriculture.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to support sustainable practices, improve the efficiency of machinery on the farm and help producers manage their infield operations with a sense of ease.

Why our Solution Works

Our approach to farm logistics starts with path planning and the automation of equipment in the field. We’ve proven that by using our software, optimizing machine paths and operational decisions influences the overall cost of each activity and action taken in the field. When equipment is at the right place, at the right time it becomes more efficient and effective; saving producers money during every stage of production.

In addition to having full control over in-field operations, our solution also allows growers to enhance their operation without any additional hardware - it’s as simple as pushing guidance lines directly to OEM platforms.

Our Story

Verge was born after acquiring First Pass; an integrated complex computational algorithm from Coutt’s Agro (a 100,000 acres farming operation based in Kindersley, SK). Acquiring First Pass was only the beginning technical journey and provided us the ability to improve challenges related to operational planning.

Next, we recruited an exceptional team made up of rocket scientists, innovative entrepreneurs, farmers and dealmakers to work on developing the world's first optimized farm logistics software centralized around path planning and the automation of in-field operational decision making.

The concept of developing an interactive tool that equips growers to create optimal path plans was our first step towards innovating and accelerating traditional farming. While most companies have been focused on increasing production; we have always been obsessed with increasing productivity across the entire farm.

We pride ourselves on working with local growers and large operations around the world to help improve their unique and diverse farm operations. Our customer’s success stories are what keep us motivated and continuously innovating.
